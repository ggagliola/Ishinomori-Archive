Topic Description:

The purpose of this website is to act as an archive or “fan-website” for the lesser known shows of Japanese artist and television writer Shotaro Ishinomori, famous for being the creator of the Kamen Rider and Super Sentai franchises. While those shows are still running today and carrying on his legacy, many of his other projects were not as successful and are less fondly remembered today. This website is designed to act as a beacon to shine light on some of his forgotten shows, including the plots, their legacies, and their inevitable fates in the realm of obscurity.

Site structure plan:

This website should have a central main page to welcome the viewer to the website. It should be the same style and feel as an early 2000’s or late 90’s fanpage for obscure topics like this one. Each page can focus on one topic or show. There’s a few that would be more content rich than others, but the primary shows I want to write about are as follows:

Android Kikaider (1972) and Kikaider 01 (1973)

Henshin Ninja Arashi (1972)

Inazuman (1973-74)

Robot Detective (1973)

Kaiketsu Zubat (1977)

Akumaizer 3 (1975)

Each page could include the basic plot premises, behind the scenes content, the production, as well as reception and legacy for the show itself. I will admit, this project is somewhat self-obsessive appealing to one of my interests, but I think I am able to effectively write about this in detail.
