Kaiketsu Zubat - Story

The series starts off with a murder. A man named Goro Asuka is mysteriously killed by the criminal organization Dakker, which rather than being a group of horrible monsters, is rather a gang with sharply dressed hitmen in their place. Left in the aftermath of the attack is his best friend and private detective, Ken Hayakawa. After his death, Ken takes hold of the mysterious equipment that Goro invented. A flying car and power suit, which he dubs the Zubat Suit and Zubat car. The name “Zubat” comes from ズバッと, the sound of something being hit right on target. With his newfound powers, Ken makes it up to his fallen friend by traveling across the country to small towns and rural communities to protect them from Dakker, and to deliver justice where the government cannot.

Dressed in his red and black cowboy hat, he travels with a guitar, hiding inside of it the Zubasuit and his equipment. The suit was originally designed for space exploration, but is now used to serve his justified cause. Rather than using a god’s blessing or an advanced belt to transform, Ken has to manually put on the suit and the mask to transform fully.

Villains reveal themselves slowly, as every episode usually features a single head criminal, a bouncer, and their henchmen, a yojimbo that are terrorizing a group of people. Using the enhanced strength and speed provided by the suit, as well as his bullet deflecting whip and iconic red dagger, Ken is able to easily defeat his opponents. Rather than them exploding or dying in some way, they are almost always arrested or taken in by local authorities for justice.

At the end of the series, the leader of Dakker reveals himself to be a grotesque man not afraid to show his whole face, Leader L. After a long and tedious battle, his bodyguards are all defeated. Thinking his end is near, he reveals that it was in fact not him who killed Goro Asuka, and that he is not the true leader of Dakker. Leaving our hero with more questions than answers, Leader L is taken into custody, as the series comes to an end at the 32nd episode.
