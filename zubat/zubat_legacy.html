Kaiketsu Zubat - Legacy

Despite the show’s popularity and positive feedback from critics, the show was never picked up for a second season or renewed for any spin-off or continuation. The story unfortunately ends at the final episode.

It is however, not without it’s mentions every few years. Most recently in 2025 with the current final Super Sentai season, No. 1 Sentai Gozyuger, Zubat himself is honored in multiple ways both by the heroes and the villain.
