Robot Detective - Main Page

Stemming from a shorter lived Manga by Ishinomori, Robot Detective, sometimes called Robot Detective K, only ran for about 3 volumes before the series was ended. However, executives at Toei seemed intrigued by the concept and the unique story it told, and as such, it was greenlit for a roughly 30 episode series that ran in 1973 alongside its more popular brothers, Kamen Rider V3 and Kikaider 01. Both of these properties, surprisingly, were sequels to the already massively popular series of his. At this time in Japan, police dramas were popular among people of all ages, usually involving elements of surprise and mystery, thus inspiring the creation of this series.
