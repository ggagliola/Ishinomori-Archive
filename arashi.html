Henshin Ninja Arashi 0

While many tokusatsu shows at the time focused on the present (ie, the 1970s, 1980s,) very few actually are set in different time periods. Even today, there are only a few examples of shows that are considered periodical. Similarly, many of Ishinomori’s stories are focused on technology driven and based around cyborgs and brilliant inventors. This series flips that status. Set in the early Edo period (1601 to 1868), Henshin Ninja Arashi takes all of the usual tropes of weekly villains, disgusting and grotesque monsters, and presents a total contrast to the usual plot point by changing what makes so many of his stories so interesting.
