Android Kikaider & Kikaider 01

As previously mentioned, many of Ishinomori’s creations come from his work as a mangaka. Many of the live action tokusatsu shows produced were often based off of his drawings or stories he would publish in his works, often sometimes serving as showcases for his abstract designs. When asked about his favorite design for a magazine published in 2004, he often considered Kikaider his magnum opus, a somewhat controversial one, as producers at Toei believed his transparent and robotic pieces would scare children.

The main design choice was intended to be dynamic. Inspired by anatomical human bodies, the blue half represents a righteous and justified heart, while the red half stands as an evil consciousness to represent imperfection.

This series would also become the first appearance of Hakaider, an evil android serving as the nemesis of Kikaider. He would quickly become a fan-favorite character, often compared to Darth Vader due to his unique helmet shape and signature black color scheme.
