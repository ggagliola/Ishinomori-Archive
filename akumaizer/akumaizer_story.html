Akumaizer 3 - Story

Inside the earth, deep beneath the crust in the molten core, lies a civilization of all the world’s evil. The Akuma Clan is a vast empire of demons and monsters who secretly plot to destroy human civilization and enslave the human race to power their wicked machinery.

The opposite logic is applied to monsters. They usually have no human counterpart that they transform from, and most of the time they’re not designed to be likable or friendly in the slightest. They need to be gruesome and grotesque, and kick puppies for fun. This design philosophy can get overwhelming however, as during a 30-50 episode series, the artist needs to design a multitude of creatures, especially operating with the “monster of the week” principle, where they’re usually defeated and explode by the end of the 20 minute episode.

We’re first introduced to Xavitan, a swordsman and defector from the Akuma Clan due to the fact that his father was a demon, and his mother was a human, who was killed in front of him by Mezalord, the boss of the Akuma Clan. After Xavitan’s rebellion causing his exile, a strike team is sent after him to finish him off after his exile. Of this group, is the vain and foppish sharpshooter, aptly named Evil, and the dim-witted water demon harnessing the ability to transform into a bird, Gabra, armed with his mace. The two successfully capture and plan to execute Xavitan, but after the tides are turned, Xavitan spares their lives. Moved by such heroism and kindness, the three combine their forces to defeat the Akuma Clan. Armed with their transforming Garado Swords, the three mirror the famous Three Musketeers as they battle their way to victory.
