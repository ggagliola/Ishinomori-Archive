Robot Detective - Legacy

While largely forgotten today, as are most of Ishinomori’s less popular works, Robot Detective lives on in the form of occasional references and nods to the series.

Near the beginning of 2014, the team behind popular manga Linebarrels of Iron announced a revival series of the original manga, deciding to focus more on the mystery of K and the grittiness of his career as a detective. The visuals released promised a fresh take on the series, but unfortunately, no manga ever came to fruition, and the project has been abandoned since the initial teaser.

Surprisingly, in 2023 with the release of Hideaki Anno’s Shin Kamen Rider, diehard fans of Ishinomori’s work got a glimpse at a reimagining of K. Dropping the detective role, K, once called J apparently, now plays the role of the advisor and messenger to I, the AI that dictates the monsters of the terroristic SHOCKER organization, hellbent on world domination through secret infiltration of different societal aspects. Fans were delighted and surprised to see the reimagined K.
